Enter the cursed city

The heavens have fallen. Your curse is the only weapon left. Survive the waves, collect orbs, chain your abilities, and break the celestial gate before the city is consumed. Move with WASD or arrows, attack with the mouse, dodge with Shift, and cast Q/E/R for devastating techniques.

Controls: Move • Attack • Dodge • Q/E/R • F Ultimate • C Curse • V Seal Tip: the curse is powerful but it eats health unless you manage your seal.
